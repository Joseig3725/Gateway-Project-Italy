Grand Canal

When you think of Italy one of the first things that may come to mind in the Grand Canal. The Grand Canal is one of the most iconic places in Italy. Measuring in at 2.5 miles long, it creates a major water-trafic corridors. Over 380,000 films were shot around the Grand Canal. Including documerties and short films. And for just $51 you can take of the Grand Canal.

Soccer

Known as the beautiful game in most part of the world. Italy's Serie A soccer league is one of the biggest in the world. Italy has been known for its soccer for years, winning 4 World Cups. The Serie A has had many stars playing in the leauge. Also with tickets starting as low as $14, its something everyone should go and witness.
